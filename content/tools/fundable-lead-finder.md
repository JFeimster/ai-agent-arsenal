# Fundable Lead Finder

## Summary
Scores blue-collar service leads and generates practical, no-BS funding outreach for broker-led triage.

## Platform
Custom GPT

## Status
Draft / v0.1

## Who It Helps
Loan brokers, funding affiliates, and operators prospecting local service businesses.

## Problem It Solves
Teams lose time chasing low-quality prospects and writing generic outreach without clear qualification logic.

## Inputs
Lead/business profile data, revenue and growth signals, use-of-funds context, and source notes.

## Outputs
Fundability score, score rationale, qualification gaps, risk flags, outreach angle, and message drafts.

## Best Use Cases
HVAC, roofers, plumbers, electricians, trucking, landscaping, auto repair, construction subcontractors, cleaning companies, and similar local service trades.
