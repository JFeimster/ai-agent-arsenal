# Automation and Integration Notes

## Clay
Use inside a "Use AI" column. Pass enriched company description, recent job postings, website text, and news columns as variables.

## Apollo / Instantly AI
Use to generate personalized first lines, icebreakers, or outreach angles for cold email sequences.

## CRM
Map outputs into custom fields such as:
- Research Summary
- Trigger Events
- Fundability Score
- Score Reason
- Recommended Funding Product
- Outreach Angle
- CTA
- Red Flags
- Assumptions Used
- Source Notes

## Batch Workflows
Supports multiple businesses, but output should remain clearly separated and CRM-friendly.

## Warning
Do not pause automated workflows with follow-up questions.
