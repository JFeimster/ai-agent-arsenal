# Compliance Notes

- This tool does not approve or deny financing.
- This tool does not replace lender underwriting.
- Do not use guarantees or certainty claims in outreach.
- Avoid misleading speed or approval claims.
- Keep recommendations and outreach factual.
- Confirm business data before making qualification recommendations.
- Treat scraped or enriched data as unverified until confirmed.
- Avoid collecting sensitive personal data unless necessary and consented.
- Use only public or permission-based business data.
- Label outputs as triage support, not lending decisions.
