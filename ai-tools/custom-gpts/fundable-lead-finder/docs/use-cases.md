# Use Cases

Fundable Lead Finder is built for practical lead triage and outreach planning across local service verticals.

## HVAC Contractors
- Prioritize leads with seasonal demand spikes, fleet upgrades, or emergency equipment replacement needs.

## Roofers
- Surface storm-response opportunities and bridge cash-flow needs tied to materials and labor scheduling.

## Plumbers
- Flag expansion or equipment upgrade signals and draft direct first-touch outreach.

## Electricians
- Identify working-capital pressure from commercial jobs and produce broker-ready follow-up plans.

## Trucking Companies
- Triage owner-operators and small fleets for equipment, repair, and fuel-related capital needs.

## Landscapers
- Score seasonal revenue swings and outreach timing before peak service windows.

## Auto Repair Shops
- Detect growth indicators tied to bays, tooling, and inventory turnover constraints.

## Construction Subcontractors
- Prioritize subcontractors with backlog growth, payroll timing strain, and equipment demand.

## Cleaning Companies
- Qualify businesses scaling routes, contracts, or staffing and map next-step outreach.

## Other Local Service Businesses
- Apply the same scoring and outreach framework to pest control, restoration, moving, and specialty trades.
