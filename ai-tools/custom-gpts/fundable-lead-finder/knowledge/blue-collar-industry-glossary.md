# Blue-Collar Industry Glossary

**File:** `blue_collar_industry_glossary.md`
**Owner GPT:** Fundable Lead Finder
**Purpose:** Gives the GPT trade-native vocabulary so research, trigger analysis, and especially outreach sound like a knowledgeable peer — not a banker. Drop the right terms in and the message instantly reads as "one of us."

> **How to use:** When writing for a given trade, prefer its native terms over generic business language. Use 1-2 naturally per message — don't stuff jargon.

---

## General Operator Terms (All Trades)

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Crew** | A work team; "running three crews" = three teams in the field |
| **Backlog** | Booked work not yet completed; signals demand |
| **Booked out** | Scheduled solid for weeks — strong demand signal |
| **Dispatch** | Coordinating crews/trucks to jobs |
| **Job** | A single project/contract |
| **Ticket / ticket size** | Dollar value of a job |
| **Net-30/60/90** | Payment terms; customer pays in 30/60/90 days |
| **Draw / draw schedule** | Staged payments on a project as milestones hit |
| **Float** | Carrying costs before getting paid |
| **Sub / subs** | Subcontractors |
| **GC** | General contractor |
| **Bid / estimate** | Quote for a job |
| **Markup** | Margin added over cost |
| **Punch list** | Final fixes before a job closes out |

## Roofing
- **Squares** (100 sq ft of roof), **tear-off**, **underlayment**, **flashing**, **pitch**, **decking**, **supplement** (insurance add-on), **adjuster**, **ACV/RCV** (insurance value terms), **storm chaser** (transient competitor).
- *Outreach flavor:* "materials before the supplement clears," "crews booked through storm season."

## HVAC
- **Units / condensers / air handlers**, **tonnage**, **SEER rating**, **install vs. service side**, **maintenance agreements (PMAs)**, **refrigerant**, **load calc**, **changeout**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "cash tied up in units," "keeping installers busy through the cooling peak."

## Plumbing
- **Service trucks**, **jetter**, **camera/scope**, **rough-in**, **fixtures**, **callbacks**, **on-call**, **commercial vs. residential**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "floating parts on net-60 commercial work."

## Electrical
- **Panels**, **service upgrade**, **rough-in**, **whips**, **gear** (switchgear), **copper costs**, **permits**, **journeymen/apprentices**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "copper and panel costs upfront before the draw."

## Concrete / Paving / Excavation
- **Pour**, **forms**, **rebar**, **cure time**, **yards** (of concrete), **mix**, **grading**, **site work**, **dirt work**, **mobilization**, **spread** (paving crew + equipment).
- *Outreach flavor:* "big pour means big materials before the draw catches up."

## Trucking / Logistics
- **Tractor / power unit**, **trailer / reefer / flatbed / dry van**, **lane**, **load**, **deadhead**, **broker**, **factoring**, **fuel surcharge**, **ELD**, **owner-operator**, **CDL**, **detention**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "getting paid on loads instead of waiting on the broker," "fuel eating the margin per mile."

## Towing
- **Wrecker**, **flatbed/rollback**, **heavy-duty / light-duty**, **impound lot**, **rotation list**, **PPI (private property impound)**, **hook**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "a new wrecker working instead of parked."

## Landscaping / Lawn Care
- **Mowers (zero-turns)**, **skid steer**, **crews/routes**, **mulch/installs**, **maintenance contracts**, **hardscape**, **spring cleanup**, **trailers**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "gearing up crews and equipment before the contracts pay."

## Restoration
- **Mitigation**, **air movers / dehus (dehumidifiers)**, **containment**, **carrier** (insurer), **adjuster**, **Xactimate** (estimating software), **cat (catastrophe) work**, **water/fire/mold remediation**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "fronting equipment and labor, then waiting 90 days on the carrier."

## Tree Service
- **Climber**, **bucket truck**, **chipper**, **crane work**, **takedown**, **stump grinding**, **storm work**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "turning down storm work because you're tapped on chippers."

## Moving
- **Box trucks**, **loads**, **long-haul vs. local**, **packing crews**, **storage (SIT)**, **binding estimate**.
- *Outreach flavor:* "summer rush means more trucks and seasonal hands."

## Anti-Patterns (Banker Words to Avoid)

❌ "capital deployment," "financial solutions," "business optimization," "liquidity event," "scalable growth strategy," "facilitate funding," "leverage our products."

✅ Replace with: "cash," "covering payroll," "buying trucks/materials," "getting paid faster," "gearing up," "funding the job."
