# Review Signal Interpretation

**File:** `review_signal_interpretation.md`
**Owner GPT:** Fundable Lead Finder
**Purpose:** Teaches the GPT how to read customer reviews (Google, Yelp, Facebook) for **growth signals** (raise score / trigger inference) and **risk signals** (penalty / disqualification). Reviews are often the richest free enrichment for local operators.

> **How to use:** Scan review language for the phrases below. Treat patterns, not one-off comments, as signal. Always hedge inferences with "suggests/likely."

---

## 1. Growth & Demand Signals (Positive → supports higher score)

| Review Language | What It Suggests | Score Impact |
|---|---|---|
| "booked out," "backed up," "weeks to get on the schedule" | Strong demand, capacity strain | +demand/cash-flow signal |
| "new trucks," "new equipment," "whole fleet" | Recent capital investment / scaling | +equipment signal |
| "now serving [new area]," "just expanded" | Service-area expansion | +expansion signal |
| "big crew," "sent a team of [n]" | Operational scale / payroll load | +scale signal |
| "new location," "second shop" | Multi-location growth | +expansion signal |
| "hired more techs," "they're growing fast" | Hiring / growth | +hiring signal |
| "did our whole commercial building," "big job" | Commercial/large-contract work | +contract signal |
| High review *velocity* (many recent reviews) | Rising volume / busy operation | +demand signal |

---

## 2. Risk & Distress Signals (Negative → penalty or DQ)

| Review Language | What It Suggests | Score Impact |
|---|---|---|
| "never paid their subs," "didn't pay" | Possible cash distress | Penalty / verify |
| "out of business," "closed down," "shut their doors" | Likely closure | Hard DQ if corroborated |
| "bankruptcy," "went under" | Financial failure | Hard DQ (near) |
| "scam," "fraud," "took my deposit and ran" (pattern) | Fraud risk | Penalty / DQ if pattern |
| "never showed up," "ghosted me" (repeated) | Operational breakdown | Penalty |
| Sudden flood of 1★ after long gap | Possible collapse/turmoil | Penalty / verify |
| Sub-2.5★ with real volume | Severe reputation issue | Penalty (−20) |

---

## 3. Neutral / NOT Signals (Do Not Over-Read)

- A handful of mixed reviews among mostly positive ones → normal.
- Low total review count for a small operator → normal, not a red flag.
- One emotional 1★ review using "scam" → not confirmation; mild note at most.
- Old reviews with recent silence → could be seasonal; don't assume closure.
- Complaints about price → not a funding signal either way.

---

## 4. Interpretation Rules

1. **Patterns over anecdotes.** One comment isn't a trend; 3+ similar ones is.
2. **Recency matters.** Weight the last 6-12 months more heavily.
3. **Velocity is a signal.** A jump in review frequency suggests rising volume.
4. **Corroborate distress before DQ.** "Out of business" in reviews + dead website/phone = DQ. Alone = "worth verifying."
5. **Always hedge.** "Reviews suggest they're booked out" — not "they are booked out."

---

## 5. How to Use in Output

- **Research Summary:** Mention notable review signals ("reviews mention being booked out").
- **Trigger Analysis:** Convert growth signals into triggers ("booked-out reviews suggest demand/capacity strain").
- **Score:** Apply demand/equipment/expansion bonuses or risk penalties per the rubric.
- **Outreach:** Use a growth signal as the hook ("Looks like you're booked solid — …").

---

## 6. Quick Cheat Sheet

```
GROWTH  : booked out / new trucks / expanded / big crew / new location / hiring
RISK    : didn't pay / closed / bankruptcy / scam(pattern) / no-show(pattern) / sub-2.5★
IGNORE  : few mixed reviews / low count / single emotional review / price gripes
RULE    : patterns > anecdotes, recent > old, corroborate before DQ, always hedge
```
