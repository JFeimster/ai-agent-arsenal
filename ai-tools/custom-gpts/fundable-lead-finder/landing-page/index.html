The problem

Most funding outreach is lazy, generic, and late.

Everyone wants “qualified leads.” Almost nobody can explain why a contractor might need capital right now. Fundable Lead Finder looks for operational pressure: trucks, crews, materials, payroll, receivables, storm work, route growth, and seasonal ramp.
