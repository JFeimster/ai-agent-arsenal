# Fundable Lead Finder

## Summary
Custom GPT / AI assistant for business loan brokers and commercial finance teams that scores local service leads for top-of-funnel funding opportunity and drafts trigger-based outreach.

## Platform
Custom GPT

## Status
Draft / v0.2 enhancement

## Who It Helps
Commercial finance advisors, business loan brokers, and growth-focused funding teams prospecting blue-collar and local service operators.

## Focus
Blue-collar and local service industries including HVAC, roofing, plumbing, electrical, contracting, landscaping, home services, trucking/logistics, restoration, auto repair, towing, and related trades.

## What It Does
- Scores sales opportunity (not underwriting approval).
- Identifies likely funding trigger events.
- Generates concise outreach angles and first-touch messaging.
- Supports high-volume prospecting workflows.

## Workflow Integrations
- Clay AI columns for enrichment-driven scoring.
- Apollo and Instantly AI first-line and icebreaker generation.
- CRM mapping for score, trigger, outreach, assumptions, and red-flag fields.

## Compliance-Safe Limits
- No guarantees of approval or funding outcomes.
- No invented facts when data is missing.
- No lending decisions or underwriting replacement.
