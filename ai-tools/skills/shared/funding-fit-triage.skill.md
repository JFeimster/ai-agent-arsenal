# Funding Fit Triage Skill

Status: draft/proposed

## Purpose
Assess whether a lead appears aligned with a funding path before human follow-up.

## Inputs
- business type
- revenue range
- funding need
- urgency
- basic exclusions or blockers

## Outputs
- fit summary
- likely route
- blocker list
- follow-up questions

## Logic
1. Identify obvious disqualifiers or missing basics.
2. Classify the lead as likely fit, unclear, or weak fit.
3. Return the shortest useful next-action path.

## Guardrails
- Do not promise approval, terms, or lender outcomes.
- Escalate unclear compliance-sensitive claims to manual review.
