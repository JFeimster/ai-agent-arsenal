# Partner Skill Roadmap

> Source: JFeimster/ai-agent-library (skills/partner-skill-roadmap.md). Imported/adapted for ai-agent-arsenal on 2026-05-30.


## Objective

Build skills that help affiliate partners, referral partners, CPAs, business brokers, consultants, and strategic partners generate better referrals, understand the offer, and follow up without sounding like a LinkedIn pitch deck that escaped containment.

1. Partner Game Plan Skill

5. CPA Referral Opportunity Skill

8. Referral Source Attribution Skill

## Partner audiences

- funding affiliates
- loan brokers
- CPAs
- bookkeepers
- business brokers
- real estate professionals
- consultants
- local business connectors
- strategic partners with SMB audiences

## Recommended app integrations

- partner recruitment landing pages
- partner onboarding forms
- partner dashboards
- partner resource hubs
- referral tracking pages
- broker profile pages
- strategic partner co-branded landing pages

## Recommended outputs

Each partner skill should produce:

- partner-safe explanation
- recommended next action
- swipe copy where relevant
- CRM/source attribution notes
- suggested resources/tools
- public/private visibility classification

## Guardrails

- Do not promise partner income.
- Do not overstate commissions.
- Do not expose backend provider routing.
- Do not create referral fee language that sounds like a suitcase full of cash changed hands behind a Waffle House.
- Use clear disclosure language where applicable.
